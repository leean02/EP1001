What is Digital Fabrication?

This elective is organised around a self-directed project where students develop design thinking and prototyping skills. Students will be guided through a design thinking process followed by the development of a functional prototype. Students will apply design and digital fabrication process for developing a functional prototype that integrates these processes. Students will practise these skills in a design studio setting. The elective will teach students techniques to design and create their projects enabling them with skills such as Computer Aided Modelling, 3D Printing, Lazer Cutting and coding.

Why Digital Fabrication?

I have always been super inclined to learning how to bring my ideas into reality. By joining this elective, I am provided the platform to to learn how to develop the skills and knowledge to do so. Furthermore, I will be able to not only learn but also use equipments that are quite expensive outside of school. I hope to be able to use the skills I pick up from Digital Fabrication in the future to develop and create unique ideas that may possibly have a positive impact to society.
